About Us

Sunrise Café started in 2020 as a passion project to bring quality coffee and homemade pastries to our community. We believe in warm smiles, cozy spaces, and freshly brewed everything.

Our café is a favorite spot for students, freelancers, and neighbors looking for a quiet, comfortable space to enjoy great food and friendly faces.
